01 The Experience

Experience the
product

Before exploring the design process, visitors are invited to experience Evopia firsthand. The desktop and mobile previews showcase the final product as an interactive website, allowing the interface to be explored exactly as it was intended.

02 The Visuals

Designing for
better rest

Evopia was inspired by the rituals surrounding quality sleep. Through imagery, colour and material references, I established a calm visual language that communicates comfort, warmth and restoration. These early explorations became the emotional foundation for the final interface.

03 The Inspiration

Building the
foundation

Before introducing visual styling, I focused on establishing a clear information architecture. Low-fidelity wireframes explored navigation patterns, product discovery and purchase flows, allowing functionality to be refined independently from aesthetics.
