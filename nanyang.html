01 Overview

Understanding
the brand

Nanyang Express is a Malaysian Chinese café located within Melbourne's Chinatown precinct. The rebrand aimed to transform an existing generic visual identity into a culturally grounded brand that celebrates the warmth of traditional kopitiam culture while remaining relevant to today's urban dining landscape.

02 The Style

Defining the
direction

Before developing the identity, I explored multiple visual directions inspired by Malaysian café culture. Each moodboard investigated a different interpretation of heritage, from nostalgic street references and playful mascot systems to hand-drawn illustrative styles.

03 The Logo

Shaping the
identity

Developing the identity required balancing heritage with contemporary appeal. Through sketching, logo exploration, typography testing and colour studies, the visual system gradually evolved into a distinctive identity centred around the iconic kopi cup.

  2. 03Logo Iterations

04 The Brand

Building the
brand system

The final identity was developed as a flexible design system rather than a standalone logo. Typography, colour, bilingual lockups and application rules were carefully defined to ensure consistency across packaging, signage, menus and digital media while preserving the character of traditional Malaysian café branding.

05 The Outcome

Bringing the
brand to life

The visual identity extends beyond printed guidelines into everyday customer experiences. From takeaway packaging and storefront signage to menus and promotional materials, each application demonstrates how the brand creates a recognisable and cohesive presence across multiple touchpoints.

06 The Reflection

Looking back

Rebranding Nanyang Express deepened my understanding of designing for cultural authenticity. Rather than relying on familiar visual stereotypes, the project challenged me to build an identity rooted in genuine Malaysian Chinese café traditions while ensuring it remained contemporary, scalable and relevant within Melbourne's multicultural dining landscape.
