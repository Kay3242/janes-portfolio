01 Overview

Building the
experience

An integrated visual system for one of Joey Yap's largest annual events, featuring signage, information boards, directional graphics, consultation areas and promotional materials. The goal was to create a premium event experience while maintaining consistency across every visitor touchpoint.

02 The Concept

Honouring
tradition

Inspired by traditional Chinese symbolism, the visual language combines auspicious reds, ornamental gold details and floral motifs with contemporary typography. The system modernises cultural aesthetics while maintaining the premium identity associated with Joey Yap's events.

03 The Wayfinding

Guiding every
step

Large-format destination signs help visitors navigate between bookstores, consultation areas, priority stations, collection counters and lounges. Clear visual hierarchy allows information to be understood quickly within a busy event environment.

04 The Information

Informing the
visitor

Instructional posters transform complex event processes into clear, sequential steps. Through icons, hierarchy and QR code integration, visitors can register, make payment and collect their reports with minimal confusion.

  1. 08Bazi Station Signage
  2. 09QR Code
05 The Details

Framing the
experience

Beyond wayfinding, large-format archways and decorative installations were designed to transform transitional spaces into memorable event moments. Inspired by traditional Chinese motifs, these structures welcomed visitors while reinforcing a cohesive visual identity throughout the venue.

06 The Reflection

Designing at
scale

Working on this project strengthened my understanding of environmental graphics and large-scale event branding. Beyond aesthetics, every design decision considered readability, navigation and consistency across numerous deliverables produced within real production timelines.
